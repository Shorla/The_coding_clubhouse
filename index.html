Dream Beyond Limits

A hands-on outreach program that selects three promising students and one teacher from each participating school. We inspire them through coding sessions and leave behind our Code Equity curriculum to help the entire school continue learning.

Teacher Training & Youth Empowerment

We equip teachers and local youth with the skills to teach coding confidently. This initiative not only creates jobs but builds a community of trained educators who champion digital literacy.

Virtual Summer Classes

Our annual summer program brings tech education to even more children through online classes. With four engaging tracks — including web development, game design, AI, and computer science basics — every child finds something to explore.
