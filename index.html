Dream Beyond Limit

Dream Beyond Limits is our flagship outreach initiative. We partner with schools in underrepresented communities to deliver hands-on coding sessions for children and a dedicated training session for one teacher per school. We leave behind our Code Equity Curriculum, ensuring the learning continues long after we're gone.
