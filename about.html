At Coding Clubhouse, we're reimagining what access to tech education looks like, we're a non-profit committed to giving every child — especially those in underrepresented communities — the opportunity to explore, learn and create with technology. We do this through outreach events like Dream Beyond Limits, Comprehensive teacher training, Summer coding classes and our Code Equity curriculum, designed to stay with schools long after we leave.
